Concepts

What is MIDI

  • MIDI (short for Musical Instrument Digital Interface) is a technical standard that describes a communications protocol, digital interface, and electrical connectors that connect a wide variety of electronic musical instruments, computers, and related audio devices. A single MIDI link can carry up to sixteen channels of information, each of which can be routed to a separate device.

    MIDI carries event messages that specify notation, pitch, velocity, vibrato, panning, and clock signals (which set tempo). For example, a MIDI keyboard or other controller might trigger a sound module to generate sound produced by a keyboard amplifier. MIDI data can be transferred via midi cable, or recorded to a sequencer to be edited or played back.

    A file format that stores and exchanges the data is also defined. Advantages of MIDI include small file size, ease of modification and manipulation and a wide choice of electronic instruments and synthesizer or digitally-sampled sounds.

About the application

What is MIORC

  • MIORC is a web application that allows you to create an online orchestra
    You can chose from a list of over 70 instruments and create your own composition.
    After you've created your song you can start forming an orchestra , with your composition and many others written by other players .

How to use MIORC

  • The application has 2 main pages:

    • The mixer page

      Here you will compose your songs

    • Mixer Structure

      A basic description of the page

      Here you will see 3 main components

      • Navigation menu

        The menu which will help you navigate through the application . By clicking it , the menu will expand

        By opening it you will see 4 buttons (ATM)
        • 1-Home button - will take you back to the main page
        • 2-Mixer button - will take you to the mixer page
        • 3-Conductor button - will take you back to the orchestra page
        • 4-Log-out/Log-in - will allow you to login/logout

      • Mixer controls

        Those will help you to interact with your song. Here you will see 6 controls

        • 1-Play button - want to hear how your song sound , just click it
        • 2-Save button - will save your song to our server
        • 3-Song name - the name which your song will be saved by
        • 4-BPM - play is to slow , rise the BPM ( Max of 10)
        • 5-Instruments - bored of the old piano try a new funky instrument
        • 6- List with all your old compositions , feel you could've done better? just load it up and edit it.

      • Note sheet

        The main actor of this play. Here you can chose your notes

        • First you will see the squares , each represents a note , by clicking it you cant listen the note , dont like it just click it again
        • As you can see the sheet is pretty small , what about that ? If you want to make it bigger it's simple add a note (square with a * symbol) on the last columns and BAM more notes to add
    • The orchestra page

      Here you will mix all the song you want

    • Orchestra Structure

      A basic description of the orchestra page

      Here you will see 2 components

    • Active song list

      The list contains the active songs which can be played
      Components:

    • 1-Start/Pause button - will play/pause all the active songs
    • 2-Stop button - will stop all playing songs
    • 3-BPM slider - will change the speed at which the songs are played
    • 4-Log-out/Log-in - will allow you to login/logout
    • 5-List with the name of the active songs
    • 6-Side card with information about the song
    • 1-Visual representation of the songs instrument
    • 2-Volume slider
    • 3-Mute button
